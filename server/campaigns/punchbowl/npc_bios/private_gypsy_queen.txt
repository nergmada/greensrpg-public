Conversation starter: "There is something evil waking up here, around us. Can you feel it?"

This basin is more than a basin, it is a living tomb, a prison. Designed to trap an evil driven here long ago, by human civilisations that pre-date even the earliest empires of Europe. 

Though most of their reminders are gone, there are still some remanants of this desperate effort to survive. North of this camp, there are caverns, and inside you'll find a druid shrine and a hermit who protects it. He can tell you more.

There is a camp to the west however, it is full of violent and dangerous thugs, take this map so that you might avoid it.
