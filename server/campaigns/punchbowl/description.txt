August 1859, you've been invited to attend the nuptials of Lady Eleanor Arthur, daughter of Lord Arthur, the 3rd Earl of Surrey, and Lt-Col Henry Williams of the Hampshire Regiment. A lavish affair hosted at the stately Bron-y-de ("Breast of the South") House. Invited as the retinue and companions of Lt-Col Williams older brother, Lord Williams, an expeditionary and explorer, turned writer.

When Lady Arthur disappears the night before the wedding during a celebratory, you are dispatched onto the surrounding heathland to search for her and return her safely in time for her betrothal. But things are not as they seem, as you venture down the basin's edge, and into The Devil's Punchbowl...
