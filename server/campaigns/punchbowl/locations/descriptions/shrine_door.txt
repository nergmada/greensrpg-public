As you peer over the ledge and onto the continuing cavern, you see a huge door frame, to its left and right, flame torches illuminating the cavern and providing some warmths in this otherwise dark and cold space. The door itself is not remotely in keeping with British architectural styles either current or present.

The artwork and images depicted on the frame and door are more in keeping with those described and drawn by Athirai, after her experience high in the Himalayan mountains exploring the disappearing shrine.

Behind the door you can hear a single voice, someone muttering to themself
